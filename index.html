DOG Breeds by country of origin

Ever wonder where your dog's breed comes from?

  • French Bichon Frise

    • The Bichon Frise, although seen as a French breed, originated in the Spanish Canary Islands. They were often used and traded as sailing and herding dogs, however, they became gentle lap-dogs with the French during the Renaissance. Bichon Frise are especially good at learning tricks and have even become star circus performers.

  • Peruvian Hairless

    • The Peruvian Hairless known in Spanish as “Perro Sin Pelo de Peru” originates from Peru. They are a pre-Incan breed kept most commonly kept as pets. Now, they are regarded as a symbol of Peru celebrated within art and literature. Peruvian Hairless are agile, intelligent, affectionate, and very protective of their family.

  • Samoyed

    • The Samoyed better know as Smiley is from the Northwest of Russia and Western Siberia. They are know hunting, guarding their caregivers, and excellent for sledding. These dogs are also great in agility trials, carting, obedience, tracking, mushing, and herding. They are truly energetic, loving, and friendly breed.

  • Pekingese(Smiley)

    • The Pekingese also know as the Lion Dog or Beijing Dog is originally from China. They are a breed that used to be only owned by the members of the Chinese Imperial Palace and as such are favored by royalty as lap dogs and companions. Legend says that the Pekingese came to be when a lion and a marmoset fell in love so the Buddha allowed them to be the same size.
